Prices


Here at websolid we believe in providing as much value to you as possible. That's why we are being upfront about our pricing and not changing it based on what we think you can afford. Whilst it's hard to put a true cost on a project there is a rough guide you can use.

For landing pages you are looking at around $1,000

For a website with content managment system (wordpress or similar) you are looking at around $1,500 per page

For an e-commerce site you are looking at around $3,000 per page.

The Process


We first catch up and have a coffee, tea or virtual zoom call. This initial chat is just a friendly chat for you to find out more about us. We feel it's important that when working with a client there's a connection. If you swipe right on us and it's a match we will work with you over a few weeks/months to establish your vision for your website, your likes and dislikes and what features you need. Once we have your seal of approval, we will develop a prototype of your website and send it to you for review. We get your feedback, update and change. We then keep doing this until you are happy with the end product.
