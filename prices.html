A one-page website has all the information a customer needs to know about a business on one page. You get a navigation bar (the thing at the top of the page). This navigation bar is able to link to various elements on the page making it easier for you customers to find the content. Typically we include a call to action, services, about and gallery section for a one pager. This is great for builders, landscapers and artists who want to promote and showcase their work. An example of a one pager we have created is Landscape Professionals
